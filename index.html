Overview of the Dairy Farm Walk Condo Site Plan


The Dairy Farm Walk Condo Site Plan represents a masterclass in integrated residential design, showcasing how thoughtful planning can create harmonious living environments that maximize connection with nature while providing exceptional amenities and living spaces. This comprehensive blueprint reveals the strategic positioning of residential blocks, communal facilities, and landscaped areas across the development's prime land parcel in Singapore's coveted Bukit Timah district. The site plan demonstrates a careful balance between built structures and open spaces, with approximately 40% of the total land area dedicated to landscaping, water features, and recreational zones that create a resort-like atmosphere for residents. The architectural team has employed a north-south orientation for the majority of residential towers, minimizing heat gain from direct east-west sun exposure while maximizing natural ventilation through the prevailing winds that characterize Singapore's tropical climate. This orientation also ensures that the maximum number of units enjoy unobstructed views of the surrounding greenery, including the adjacent Dairy Farm Nature Park and the distant Bukit Timah Nature Reserve.


The Dairy Farm Walk Condo Site Plan reveals a thoughtful zoning approach that creates distinct yet interconnected areas for different activities and experiences throughout the development. The residential towers are arranged in a semi-circular configuration that creates a sense of embrace around the central facilities, with varying heights that provide visual interest while maximizing views and privacy for residents. The tallest structures are positioned toward the northern boundary, stepping down toward the south to create a terraced effect that allows optimal sunlight penetration to all areas of the development. This arrangement also creates natural sound barriers from nearby roads, with the buildings themselves shielding the central recreational areas from traffic noise and creating tranquil spaces for relaxation and social interaction. The site plan incorporates multiple entry and exit points that distribute traffic flow efficiently while maintaining security, with a grand arrival court that creates a strong sense of arrival and sets the tone for the luxury experience that awaits within.


Water features play a prominent role in the Dairy Farm Walk Condo Site Plan, with a series of interconnected pools, reflective ponds, and water gardens that create visual continuity throughout the development while providing cooling effects in Singapore's tropical climate. The centerpiece is an expansive infinity-edge swimming pool that appears to merge with the surrounding forest canopy, complemented by separate lap pools, children's water play areas, and hydrotherapy spas that cater to different preferences and age groups. These aquatic elements are strategically positioned to create stunning reflections of the surrounding architecture and landscape, enhancing the visual impact of the development while creating tranquil spaces for relaxation and recreation. The water features also serve practical functions within the site's comprehensive stormwater management system, collecting and filtering rainwater for irrigation and landscape maintenance while reducing runoff and supporting the development's sustainability objectives.


Circulation patterns within the Dairy Farm Walk Condo Site Plan have been carefully designed to create intuitive wayfinding while separating vehicular and pedestrian traffic for safety and comfort. The main vehicular access leads to a basement parking structure that accommodates residents' vehicles discreetly below ground, preserving the ground level for pedestrian-friendly landscapes and recreational spaces. A network of covered walkways connects all buildings and amenities, ensuring comfortable movement throughout the development regardless of weather conditions, with accessible routes that accommodate residents of all mobility levels. The pedestrian pathways feature varying widths and materials that subtly indicate their hierarchy and function, from broad promenades designed for social interaction to intimate garden paths that encourage exploration and discovery. These circulation routes are enhanced by thoughtful lighting design that ensures safety and security while creating dramatic nighttime effects that highlight the development's architectural features and landscape elements.


The Dairy Farm Walk Condo Site Plan incorporates extensive green spaces that create a seamless transition between the built environment and the surrounding natural landscape. Tiered gardens, verdant lawns, and densely planted perimeter buffers create layers of greenery that soften the development's visual impact while providing habitat for birds and butterflies that enhance the connection to nature. The landscape design features a carefully selected palette of native and adaptive plant species that thrive in Singapore's tropical climate while requiring minimal maintenance and irrigation. Mature trees have been preserved wherever possible and complemented by new plantings that will develop over time to create a lush, established character from the outset. These green spaces serve multiple functions beyond their aesthetic appeal, including temperature moderation, air purification, and stormwater management that support the development's environmental performance while creating healthy, restorative environments for residents to enjoy.


Residential Block Layout and Unit Distribution


The residential component of the Dairy Farm Walk Condo Site Plan comprises five distinct towers, each with its own architectural character while maintaining a cohesive design language that unifies the development. The towers are identified as Blocks A through E, with varying heights ranging from 12 to 20 stories that create a dynamic skyline while responding to site conditions and view opportunities. Block A, positioned at the northwestern corner of the site, rises to 20 stories and features predominantly three and four-bedroom premium units that capture panoramic views of the Bukit Timah Nature Reserve. Block B, centrally located along the northern boundary, stands at 18 stories and offers a mix of two and three-bedroom units with extended balconies that maximize the connection to the surrounding greenery. Block C occupies the northeastern corner at 16 stories, featuring compact one and two-bedroom units designed for young professionals and investors seeking efficient layouts without compromising on quality or amenities. Blocks D and E, positioned along the southern portion of the site, rise to 12 and 14 stories respectively, with a mix of unit types that include exclusive garden residences on the lower floors and penthouses with private roof terraces on the upper levels.


The unit distribution across the development has been carefully calibrated to meet market demand while creating diverse communities within each residential block. One-bedroom units, ranging from 45 to 60 square meters, comprise approximately 20% of the total inventory and are concentrated in Blocks C and D, catering to young professionals, investors, and those seeking pied-à-terre accommodations in this prestigious district. Two-bedroom units, ranging from 70 to 90 square meters, represent the largest segment at approximately 40% of the total inventory, distributed across all blocks with various configurations that include study options and different balcony sizes to accommodate diverse lifestyle preferences. Three-bedroom units, ranging from 100 to 130 square meters, account for approximately 30% of the inventory and are positioned primarily in Blocks A, B, and E, designed with family living in mind and featuring generous living areas and helper's rooms in selected layouts. The remaining 10% consists of premium four-bedroom units and penthouses, ranging from 150 to 200+ square meters, located exclusively on the upper floors of Blocks A and E to maximize views and privacy for these luxury residences.


The stacking plan revealed in the Dairy Farm Walk Condo Site Plan demonstrates thoughtful consideration of privacy, views, and unit mix throughout the vertical dimension of each tower. Lower floors feature larger units with extended private gardens that create a strong connection to the landscape, while middle floors offer a diverse mix of unit types that balance affordability with desirable views and amenities. Upper floors are dedicated to premium units with expansive balconies and floor-to-ceiling windows that maximize views and natural light, culminating in exclusive penthouse residences with private roof terraces and plunge pools on the topmost levels. This vertical stratification creates natural communities within each tower while ensuring that all residents enjoy quality living spaces regardless of their position within the development. The elevator core and service areas are centrally positioned in each tower to minimize corridor lengths and maximize efficiency, with separate service elevators that ensure discreet movement of maintenance personnel and delivery services without disrupting resident circulation.


The façade treatment and architectural articulation of the residential blocks create visual interest while responding to functional requirements for sun shading, privacy, and ventilation. Deep overhangs, vertical fins, and horizontal louvers protect interior spaces from direct sunlight while creating dynamic shadow patterns that animate the building exteriors throughout the day. Balconies are strategically positioned to provide outdoor living spaces while creating visual breaks in the façade that reduce the perceived mass of the buildings. The material palette combines textured concrete, glass, and metal elements with warm wood accents that reference the natural surroundings, creating a contemporary aesthetic that will age gracefully over time. Each tower features a distinctive crown element that creates a recognizable identity while contributing to the overall skyline composition of the development, with integrated lighting that creates dramatic effects during evening hours without contributing to light pollution that might impact the surrounding natural areas.


The interface between residential blocks and communal spaces has been carefully considered in the Dairy Farm Walk Condo Site Plan, with ground floor units featuring elevated terraces that create privacy buffers while maintaining visual connections to the surrounding landscape. Lobby spaces are designed as transitional zones that mediate between public and private realms, with double-height volumes, natural materials, and integrated plantings that create welcoming arrival experiences for residents and visitors. Each residential block includes dedicated amenity spaces such as reading lounges, co-working areas, and function rooms that complement the development-wide facilities while creating intimate gathering spaces for residents of each tower. These spaces are positioned to capture views and natural light while maintaining acoustic separation from residential units, ensuring that social activities do not disturb the tranquility of private living spaces. The residential blocks are connected by landscaped bridges at various levels that create opportunities for chance encounters and community building while providing alternative circulation routes that enhance the experience of moving through the development.
